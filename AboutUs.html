About JDM Cars KE

Kenya's premier destination for Japanese Domestic Market vehicles — bringing the best of Japan's roads to East Africa.

Our Story

JDM Cars KE was founded by a group of car enthusiasts who saw a gap in the Kenyan market — quality Japanese performance and reliability vehicles that weren't readily available. We started small, importing a handful of carefully selected vehicles, and quickly grew into a trusted network of suppliers, dealers, and loyal customers.

Today we connect Kenyan buyers directly with the best JDM stock across Japan, offering transparent pricing, detailed vehicle histories, and dedicated after-sale support. Every car we source is inspected, verified, and matched to the buyer's needs.

Why JDM?

Japanese Domestic Market vehicles are built to strict local standards, often featuring technology and safety specs not available in export models. They are renowned for low mileage, excellent maintenance records, and outstanding long-term reliability — making them ideal for Kenya's diverse road conditions.
